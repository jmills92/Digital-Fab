Here is all the documentation for assignment 5!

Phase 1: Planning & Design

To showcase movement, I chose to build a pair of scissors

I went on Thingiverse and found some inspiration

My strategy for the design was to use tips from Joshua's tutorial to create the base of the scissor, I used the line tool to build and connect the shape of the scissors and then created curves to soften the edges.

I then duplicated the shape, combined them and placed the screw inside. The size of the circle, for the screw measureed at 7.06mm radius.

Phase 2: Laser printing

I chose to laser print on acrylic and leveraged the materials that Joshua had available for the class in The MILL.

I decided to test the prints on cardboard first, which proved to be worth it. I made some adjustments to the size of the scissors, and then placed the acrylic in.

Printer settings:

Speed: 10

Powewr:100
